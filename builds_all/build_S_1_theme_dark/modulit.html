Modul IT

Modul IT company makes custom furniture design and manufacturing, provides system design, consults on ergonomic solutions applications and implementations. Modul IT Solutions’ knowledgeable and professional staff has worked in the Pro AV industry and within the territory for many years. From advance Audio systems to groundbreaking video conferencing equipment, Modul IT Solution’s aim is to constantly update its range, always being ahead of the rest, facilitating and supplying the latest developments in Pro AV products.
